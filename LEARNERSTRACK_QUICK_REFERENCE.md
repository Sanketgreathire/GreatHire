## 🎨 Branding Elements

### Logo Display
- Position: **Center top** of form
- Format: **GreatHire × LearnersTrack**
- LearnersTrack logo: **8 height** (auto width)
- Separator: **×** (gray color)

### Text Changes

| Element | Original | LearnersTrack Version |
|---------|----------|----------------------|
| **Login Title** | "Welcome Back!" | "Welcome to LearnersTrack" |
| **Login Subtitle** | "Find the job made for you" | "Exclusive community with GreatHire" |
| **Signup Title** | "Create your account" | "Join LearnersTrack Community" |
| **Signup Subtitle** | "Join GreatHire and find opportunities!" | "Exclusive opportunities with GreatHire!" |
